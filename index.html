Account Creation

How To Create an Account

Launch your AZ eWallet app
Tap on 'Create an Account' at the bottom of the screen
Enter your name, mobile number, civil ID number, email address and a password
Tap on 'Create Account'
You will get an OTP on the mobile number you entered
Once you enter the OTP and click on ‘Submit’
Tap on 'Create Account'
Your account will be created
